What inspired me to make this piece was an earlier project I made in my senior year of high school. As a final project, I use all my graphic design skills to create a comic book-like art piece that resembled something like an action scene or portrait of the hero with bright lines in the background as he fights and jumps around. Thus, creating the drawing machine. As you draw, you may see a resemblance to lasers or an explosion. Which was what was the direction I was going. I also choose the color red because the drawing machine looks like a video game I played before that incorporated a ton of red and black.
